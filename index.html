Why this project?

About the author

Arash Rezaei

PhD researcher at Karlsruhe Institute of Technology (KIT)

Arash works on full-waveform inversion and seismic imaging. His research focuses on the technical development of FWI, while also examining how the field evolved through the people, ideas, and discussions behind the published work.

About the project

The history that papers do not fully record

The FWI Story is an independent oral-history project that documents the development of full-waveform inversion through interviews with senior researchers and pioneers of the field.

The project records memories of early research, unsuccessful attempts, practical challenges, conference discussions, major turning points, and views on the future of FWI. These details are often missing from technical papers.

Each interview is prepared as a written historical record. Participants can review and approve the final version before any material is made public.
